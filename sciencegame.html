Grow Your Plant




🌿 Fun Plant Growth Game Rules:

- Collect the Good Ones ✅: Grab pure air and clean water to increase your count.
- Avoid the Bad Ones ❌: Stay away from poisonous air and impure water—they decrease your resources.
- Use a Bowl to Collect 🏺: Your bowl catches both good and bad elements.
- Move the Bowl 🏹: Control the bowl using arrow keys to position it effectively.
- Progressive Challenge ⏩: The falling speed of air and water increases as you progress, making it harder over time!
- Visit the Shop 🛒:
- Increase bowl speed to collect items faster.
- Upgrade your plant for better growth.
- Purchase Forcefield 🛡️: Protect yourself from impurities for 10 seconds.
- Fully Growing Your Plant 🌱:
- Grants 4 chillies 🌶️ upon full growth.
- Chillies are used to buy forcefields that last 10 seconds and protect against bad elements.
- Unlocking the Forcefield 🛡️: Available only after growing your first plant.
- Endless Growth 🔄: You can regrow your plant continuously, earning 4 chillies each time it fully grows.
